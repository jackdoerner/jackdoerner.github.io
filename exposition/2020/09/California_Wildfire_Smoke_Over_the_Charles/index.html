California Wildfire Smoke Over the Charles

Dusk falls early
A dusk
Made of faraway embers
And ash
Sea to sea
And the chill of September
At last
Though we see
We will fail to remember

That the seas don't shine
And the trees debride
As we stay inside
And we bide our time
For just one more night
One more
