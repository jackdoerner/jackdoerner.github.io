I am an assistant professor of computer science at the Univer­sity of Virginia. I study crypto­graphy, with a parti­cular int­erest in the found­ations and applic­ations of secure multi­party compu­tation.

Previously, I held postdoc positions at Brown Univer­sity, hosted by Anna Lysyan­skaya, and jointly at the Technion and Reichman Univer­sity, hosted by Yuval Ishai and Elette Boyle. My PhD was advised by abhi shelat at North­eastern Univer­sity.

I am also an artist. My work spans cinema­tography, photo­graphy, and sculpture. I often spend my free time camping, hiking, sailboat racing, scuba diving, boulder­ing, and repairing mecha­nical cameras.
